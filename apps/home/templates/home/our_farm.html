Oak Farm

Oak Farm - Growing What We Serve

Oak Farm is the production hub behind the freshness you experience at Zamar Springs Gardens. Here, we grow a wide variety of vegetables including onions and other seasonal produce used directly in our kitchen.

Our dairy production supports many of the beverages and meals served on our menu. Fresh milk from our farm is used in tea preparation, milkshakes, yoghurt production and other milk-based offerings.

Seasonal fruits harvested from Oak Farm are used to prepare fresh salads and natural fruit juices, ensuring quality and flavor consistency throughout the year.

This farm-to-table approach allows us to maintain control over quality, sustainability and freshness while supporting environmentally responsible agriculture.

Greenhouse & Nursery

Greenhouse & Tree Nursery - Growing Nature

Our greenhouse serves as the seedling incubation center for flowers, native trees and landscaping plants. Every plant is nurtured carefully from early growth to garden-ready maturity.

These seedlings are grown for sale and landscaping projects, supporting environmental sustainability, biodiversity and reforestation initiatives across Machakos and beyond.
